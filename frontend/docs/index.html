01

Overview

Butler AI: PC Automation (v6.0), also referred to as "Nexus Command Center" or "CommandCube", is a local-network, self-hosted PC automation app. It connects your Android device to a Python server (butler_server.py) running on your own PC over your home or office Wi-Fi network.

🔒

Our Privacy Philosophy

Your data is your own. The App is built on a local-first, privacy-centric architecture. We do not collect, store, transmit, or monetize any of your personal information.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. No data ever leaves your local network or reaches any third party unless you explicitly configure optional AI features described in Section 4.

02

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not operate remote servers to collect your data. Specifically, we do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data sent to our servers
  • Usage analytics, crash logs, or telemetry data
  • Chat histories or command logs

To provide core functionality, the App requires certain on-device permissions. All data processed via these permissions remains strictly local to your device.

CAMERA
QR Code Pairing Only Used solely for scanning QR codes to pair your phone with your local PC server. The camera feed is processed locally and discarded immediately. No images or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted.
LAN ACCESS
Local Network Communication Only The App communicates directly over your Local Area Network to interface with your own PC. This traffic is routed directly between your phone and your computer — it never passes through our servers.
LOCAL STORAGE
Private On-Device Storage The App uses your device's secure local storage to save preferences, scripts, and pairing configurations. This data never leaves your device and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

The following data is stored in your device's private AsyncStorage (inaccessible to other apps or any external server):

  • Server IP / Port — IP address of your PC on your local network
  • Session Token — cryptographic bearer token for authenticating with your PC server
  • Device ID — random UUID for device-lock pairing with your PC
  • Knowledge Base — automation knowledge you crawl or enter (your data, local)
  • Script Library — Python scripts you create or import (your data, local)
  • Chat History — Butler AI conversations (your data, local)
  • App Settings — configuration preferences

All locally stored data is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

05

Optional Third-Party Services

🤖

Local Ollama AI — Default & Fully Private

By default, Butler AI uses a local Ollama AI model running on your own PC. Your chat messages are sent over your local WiFi to your own PC only — nothing goes to any external server. Completely private, all processing on your hardware.

🔑

Google Gemini AI — Optional & User-Configured

If you configure a Gemini API key in Settings, Butler AI can optionally send chat messages to Google's Gemini API. Only your typed messages are sent — no names, emails, device ID, location, or personal identifiers are ever included. Default state: Disabled. Remove the key in Settings at any time to disable. Security Practices

PracticeDetails
Network scopeAll communication on your local network only (LAN — 192.168.x.x, 10.x.x.x)
Authentication64-character cryptographic Bearer tokens via secrets.token_urlsafe(64)
Timing attacksConstant-time comparison (secrets.compare_digest) prevents timing attacks
EncryptionOptional TLS/HTTPS via self-signed certificate generated by butler_server.py
Rate limiting60 requests/IP/minute to prevent abuse
Device lockPC server pairs to ONE device at a time
Script safetyDangerous patterns (os.system, eval, exec, shell=True) blocked before execution
Script limits30-second execution timeout, 64KB size limit
