PLAIN ENGLISH SUMMARY

Butler AI is a 100% self-hosted remote PC automation tool. It connects your Android phone to a Python server running on your own PC over your local Wi-Fi. We collect zero personal data. We have no servers. Your scripts, settings, and chat history never leave your home network. The Camera permission is used exclusively when you tap the QR scan button to pair with your PC — nothing is photographed or stored.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only in your phone's private app storage (Android AsyncStorage). It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external server, company, or third party:

  • Your PC's local IP address and port number
  • Session pairing token (cryptographic HMAC hash, not a password)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Python scripts you save in the Script Library
  • Execution history (command logs)
  • Knowledge Base entries (auto-generated Python reference notes)
  • Chat history with the local AI (stored only on-device)

2. Data We Never Collect

We explicitly do not collect, access, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any personal identity
  • GPS location or network-based location data
  • Device contacts, calendar entries, or call logs
  • Photos, camera images, or gallery content
  • Microphone audio or voice recordings
  • SMS messages or notification content
  • Advertising IDs or device fingerprints
  • Browser history, cookies, or web tracking data
  • Health, financial, or biometric data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic telemetry
  • Usage analytics, heatmaps, or session recordings

3. Camera Permission

CAMERA — Used Only for QR Code Pairing

Butler AI requests the android.permission.CAMERA permission for one purpose only: scanning a QR code displayed by the butler_server.py Python script running on your PC. This QR code contains your PC's local IP address and a pairing token so you do not need to type them manually.


  • Camera is only activated when you tap the "Scan QR" button on the Home screen
  • Camera is never activated in the background
  • No photos, screenshots, or images are captured, saved, or transmitted
  • QR scan data stays on-device only (the IP and token are saved locally)
  • You can pair manually by typing your IP — camera is optional

4. All Android Permissions

INTERNET
Required to communicate with your butler_server.py over your local Wi-Fi. Only LAN traffic — no internet endpoints are contacted during normal use.
CAMERA
Used only when you tap "Scan QR Code" to pair with your PC. No images stored. Never activated in background.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Checks if you are connected to Wi-Fi before attempting to reach your PC server. Prevents wasted connection attempts over cellular.
NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES
(Android 12+)
Enables the optional LAN Auto-Discover feature, which scans your local subnet via UDP broadcast to find your PC server automatically. Manual IP entry always works without this.

Permissions NOT Requested

READ_CONTACTS  ·  WRITE_CONTACTS  ·  ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION  ·  ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION  ·  RECORD_AUDIO  ·  READ_SMS  ·  SEND_SMS  ·  READ_CALL_LOG  ·  GET_ACCOUNTS  ·  READ_PHONE_STATE  ·  WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE  ·  READ_MEDIA_VIDEO  ·  READ_MEDIA_AUDIO

5. How Data Is Transmitted

All communication occurs exclusively between your Android phone and your personal PC over your local home or office Wi-Fi network (192.168.x.x, 10.x.x.x, or similar private ranges). No data is routed through the internet, our servers, or any third-party relay during standard operation.


Optional AI Relay (Φ-NEXUS Bridge): If you explicitly enable the optional cloud AI relay in Settings, Butler AI may send your typed question text to a Supabase Edge Function, which queries Google Gemini. This feature is disabled by default. No personal identity, IP address, scripts, or location is included in these requests. You can permanently disable this under Settings → Φ-NEXUS Bridge → Local-Only Mode.

No Analytics SDKs

Butler AI contains zero analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. No Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Sentry, or any tracking library is included.

7. Data Security

  • Device pairing uses HMAC-SHA256 cryptographic signatures — no plain-text passwords
  • Session tokens expire automatically after 30 days
  • Your PC server locks to one device at a time — new pairing revokes old one
  • All app data is stored in Android's private app sandbox — other apps cannot read it
  • Data is automatically wiped when you uninstall the app
  • Python scripts are executed in isolated subprocesses with a 30-second timeout
  • Built-in Safety Guard scans scripts for dangerous patterns before execution

8. Children's Privacy

Butler AI is a developer productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal information from anyone, this policy applies uniformly to all users.

9. Your Rights & Data Control

  • Access: All data is on your device — view it in-app at any time
  • Delete: Settings → Data & Storage → Clear App Cache, or simply uninstall the app
  • Export: Settings → Butler AI Knowledge Base → Export Knowledge Base (JSON)
  • Correction: Edit scripts and settings directly in the app
  • No Account: No account exists to delete — we have no user database
